How to Measure Your Feet

Step 1: Prepare
  • Wear socks you'll use with the shoes
  • Measure in the afternoon (feet swell)
  • Stand on a flat surface
Step 2: Measure
  • Place foot on paper
  • Mark longest point (heel to toe)
  • Measure width at widest point

Fitting Tips

For Athletic Shoes
  • Leave 1/2 inch space at the toe
  • Ensure heel fits snugly
  • Check arch support
  • Test flexibility
For Formal Shoes
  • Should fit snug but not tight
  • No heel slippage
  • Comfortable toe room
  • Proper arch alignment
For Casual Shoes
  • Comfortable fit all around
  • Easy to slip on/off
  • Good breathability
  • Flexible sole
Width Guide
  • Narrow: A, B, C
  • Medium: D, E
  • Wide: EE, EEE
  • Extra Wide: EEEE

Common Fitting Issues

Too Small
  • Toes feel cramped
  • Heel slippage
  • Pain in ball of foot
  • Numbness in toes
Too Large
  • Excessive heel slippage
  • Foot slides forward
  • Poor arch support
  • Unstable walking
